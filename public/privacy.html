1. Who we are

Get Biz Idea ("we", "us", "our") operates getbizidea.com. Contact us at hello@getbizidea.com.

2. What data we collect

We collect only what's necessary to provide our service:

  • Email address — when you choose to receive your results by email

  • Payment data — processed securely by Stripe. We never store card details.

3. How we use your data

  • To generate and deliver your personalised business plan

  • To process your payment (via Stripe)
  • To improve the product (anonymised analytics only)

4. Email communications

If you provide your email, we may send you your results and occasional tips. You can unsubscribe at any time using the link in any email we send.

5. Data sharing

We do not sell your data. We share data only with:

  • Stripe — payment processing

  • Supabase — secure database storage (EU region)
  • PostHog — anonymised analytics (EU region)

6. Data retention

Quiz results and email addresses are stored for up to 12 months. You can request deletion at any time by emailing hello@getbizidea.com.

7. Your rights (GDPR)

You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data. Email us at hello@getbizidea.com and we will respond within 30 days.

8. Cookies

We use minimal cookies for analytics only. No advertising or tracking cookies are used.
